Fried Potatoes Recipe

Description

The classic from southern Italy. Simple but sublime - spaghetti in a fruity tomato sauce served with parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 200g potatoes
  • tomato sauce
  • parmesan

Steps

  1. Heat 500ml of water in a pot
  2. Heat the sauce in a pot
  3. When the water boils add the potatoes
  4. When both sauce is hot and spaghetti is done serve with parmesan
